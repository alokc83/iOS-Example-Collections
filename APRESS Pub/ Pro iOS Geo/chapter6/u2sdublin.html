U2'S DUBLIN

This app shows on the Dublin map significant landmarks related to U2 rock band's story.

  • Use the Choose menu to select a specific place.
  • Click on a marker to open its balloon.
  • Scroll through the balloon content.
  • Click on the Nearest spots button to activate your location tracking and show the 2 nearest spots. Click again to stop the process.
